Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Urgon ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Urgon mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

1. Overview

Urgon is an offline-first car rental booking management app. All data entered into the App is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to external servers.

2. Information We Collect

We may store the following information locally on your device:

  • Customer names
  • Customer phone numbers
  • Booking details (dates, times, vehicles, notes)
  • Payment information (amounts, deposits, balances)

We do not collect or store this data on any remote servers.

3. How We Use Information

The information is used solely to:

  • Manage car rental bookings
  • Track payments and deposits
  • Display booking and customer details inside the App

We do not use your data for analytics, advertising, or profiling.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms.
  • We do not transmit, upload, or share your data externally.

However, please note that:

  • You are responsible for securing your device.
  • If you uninstall the App, your data may be permanently deleted.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or disclose your data to any third parties.

6. Permissions

Urgon may request limited device permissions (such as storage access) solely to:

  • Save and retrieve booking data
  • Enable app functionality

We do not access unrelated personal data.

7. Third-Party Services

Urgon does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or external data processors.

8. Your Control Over Data

Since all data is stored locally:

  • You have full control over your data
  • You can edit or delete data inside the App at any time
  • You can remove all data by uninstalling the App

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

11. Compliance

This Privacy Policy is intended to comply with applicable data protection laws and Google Play requirements.
